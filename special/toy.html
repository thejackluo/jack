Jack Luo - Toy Product Design

Week 1: Innovative Toy Concepts

Fidget Ball

Two pieces to move simultaneously towards shining LEDs within a time limit, challenging coordination and speed.

Double Pendulum Challenge

A chaotic toy using momentum to fit a two-pendulum system into a "digital mold," with adjustable game modes.

Rejuiced Tug of Energy

Teams use water guns to generate energy and move a generator, combining strategy and summer fun.

4D Hexa Blocker

A mind-bending multidimensional puzzle with twists, turns, and 4D obstacles to challenge spatial thinking.

PolyRhythm

A stationary machine with a spinning outer ring and seven buttons, combining exercise and dance for fun fitness.

Week 2: Exploring Imagination

Crazy Story Roulette

Generate stories based on different people, places, and settings. A spinner is used to select the elements that create a unique narrative.

Custom Chess

Create new rules in this modern chess variant, introducing custom pieces and obstacles on the board.

Dual Lens Mixer

A lens system with 16 possibilities, offering multiple perspectives through different lenses arranged in layers.

Combining Laser Maze

Embark on a 2D adventure in optics. The laser maze involves reflecting and combining lasers to solve puzzles.

Music Painting

Draw your music out! A creative tool that lets you visualize and "paint" music using a combination of keys and a sketchpad.

Custom Origami

Experimental origami with unique designs and folding techniques, allowing for custom creations.

Remote Ball

A ball with a gyroscope, controlled remotely, allowing the user to drive it around in different directions.
