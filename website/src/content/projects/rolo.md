Rolo is built for people whose work compounds through **trust, timing, and recall**—not pipeline stages. It is an **AI relationship intelligence** layer: one place to see who needs attention, what is coming up on the calendar, and which threads still have open loops.

## What you get

- **Search your network by intent** — Find intros, expertise, geography, or fuzzy “I met them at…” fragments instead of exact CRM keywords.
- **Meeting prep in minutes** — Briefs pull recent history, adjacent relationships, and open commitments so you walk in warm.
- **Context that stays on the person** — Notes and signals attach to relationships instead of scattering across tabs, screenshots, and DMs.

## Who it is for

Rolo explicitly ships positioning for **founders**, **investors**, **freelancers**, and **students**—the same core product translated for audiences that all hit the same failure mode: context drops, follow-up slips, and opportunities cooling off before anyone acts.

## Status

Rolo is **deployed, published, and live** at [**rolo.agentschool.io**](https://rolo.agentschool.io). The production app is in active use: capture, search, contact records, relationship timelines, meeting briefs, and Signals are shipping features—not a pre-launch landing page. The marketing site still walks through the full product story (overview, search, network views, CRM comparisons) for new visitors, but the **application itself is launched** and the roadmap now focuses on depth, performance, and trust (capture quality, ranking, integrations, and privacy) rather than a waitlist.

Rolo sits under the Agent School umbrella as the vertical bet that **relationship memory** deserves the same rigor as code and capital.
