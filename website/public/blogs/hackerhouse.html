What was the composition of the house?
The house was a 3 story house with 2 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms. The frst floor was 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The second floor was the living room, kitchen, and a huge "makeshift bedroom". The third floor was the rooftop with clear views of the central financial district. The house was located in the middle of the city, which was very convenient for us to get to places.
